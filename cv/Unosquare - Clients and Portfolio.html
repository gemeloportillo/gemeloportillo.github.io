Foundation Medicine (NASDAQ: FMI) is a molecular information company focused on fundamentally changing the way patients with cancer are treated. Their proprietary molecular information platform generates actionable genomic information about a patient’s individual cancer, enabling physicians to optimize treatments in clinical practice and enabling biopharmaceutical companies to develop targeted oncology therapies more effectively. The Company’s first clinical product, FoundationOne™, is the first commercially available comprehensive molecular information product designed for use in the routine care of patients with cancer.

Issue

Due to rapid growth, Foundation Medicine needed to augment its .NET engineering staff to develop and support core applications, cost effectively. Finding themselves short in time and resources, they needed other options to resolve the issue.

Solution

Unosquare proposed a staff augmentation model that enabled Foundation Medicine to rapidly scale-up Sr. .NET engineers in less time while mitigating costs.

Outcome

Foundation Medicine hit the ground running with 3 Sr. NET Developers and easily scaled the team to 17 who successfully support all their development initiatives today.

Gol Marketing, headquartered in Mexico, is a global sports marketing firm specializing in soccer.

Business Units include soccer marketing (consulting, sponsorships and events) and team and player analytics (development of innovative software for clubs, players and fans).

The issue

The acquisition and usage of high end video editors are expensive and cumbersome with a steep learning curve and inconsistent output. The challenge was to create consistent videos that effectively captured both video and player data.

Solution

Unosquare helped Gol Marketing to create an application that drives their core business. The project is used by a few dozen editors who categorize plays in all games for teams to identify valuable players. The system keeps a database of statistics for all players of both the MLS and the Mexican Premier Soccer League.

Outcome

All statistics are backed by corresponding videos so coaches and players can review them and assess opponents and their player lineups. The application features a composition engine that supports dimensional and motion screening for graphical elements, audio mixing and normalization, including fading effects.

ViewDo Labs is a company focused on helping increase the adoption of Yammer while lowering the risks of collaboration. Their main product offers a federated view of what is happening with Enterprise Social Networks in order to better leverage business data and intellectual property.

Issue

ViewDo Labs wanted to package an experimental device for network traffic analysis with the purpose of gathering social network usage statistics. The software needed to be multi-platform and have the ability to run on different architectures. The software also had to have the ability to be deployed as a stand-alone service or daemon running on-premises.

Solution

Using open-source technologies, Unosquare architected and implemented a service that is configurable from any remote location, has an HTML5 front-end and collects usage statistics within a local network segment.

Outcome

At the end of the day, ViewDo Labs was able to easily distribute, deploy and update the network analysis tool in a variety of platforms with ease. The tool requires little to no support as everything can be configured and retrieved remotely.
