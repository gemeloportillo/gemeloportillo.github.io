Why you will love to work at unosquare?

You'll be given the opportunity to develop your skills on amazing projects for amazing clients. In other words - doing what you love. You will join a company that doesn't obsess about rules and fixed hours but rewards you for your hard work and skill. Coffee, soft drinks and Thursday chilaquiles are provided with ease. In addition to flexible hours and a relaxed atmosphere we provide a fully equipped game room and periodic gaming nights.

In addition to being provided with top of the line computers and dual monitors, we encourage engineers to bring their own devices and try new things in our Unosquare lab.

BUSINESS ANALYST

Business Analyst

Full Time – Guadalajara or Leon

Job Description

Our business analyst will help businesses implement technology solutions in a cost-effective way by determining the requirements of a project or program, and communicating them clearly to stakeholders, facilitators and partners.


Responsibilities

-Requirements analysis.

-Planning, executing and monitoring solutions.

-Translating and simplifying requirements.

-Evaluate results.


Requirements

-Experience with any programming language.

-Understanding of systems engineering concepts.

-The ability to conduct cost/benefit analysis

-Business case development.

-Modeling techniques and methods.

-Leadership.


FUNCTIONAL TESTING

Junior Functional Tester

Full Time – Guadalajara or Leon

Job Description

Our software quality assurance engineer perform software tests to ensure quality.


Responsibilities

-Design and executive test plans on computer applications.

-Detect software failures so that defects may be discovered and corrected.

-Document anomalies and issues.

-Develop and document application test plans based on software requirements and technical specifications.

-Create meaningful error handling procedures for application code.


Requirements

-Experience developing and executing test cases, scripts, data sheets, results.

-1 year of experience.

-Strong analytical skills.

-Excellent verbal and written communication skills.
